Key Areas of Transformation: Media Mix & Creative

Media Mix Optimization

Traditional Approach

  • Historical performance analysis
  • Rule-of-thumb budget allocation
  • Quarterly rebalancing

AI-Amplified Approach

  • Multi-touch attribution modeling

Creative Performance Prediction

Traditional Approach

  • A/B testing 2-3 variants
  • Wait weeks for statistical significance
  • Limited personalization

AI-Amplified Approach

  • Predict performance before launch
  • Test hundreds of variations simultaneously
  • Dynamic creative personalization

Impact: Reduce creative waste and accelerate time-to-insight from weeks to hours.
