Implementation Roadmap

A structured, phased approach to AI integration (7-12 months)

Phase 1: Foundation

Months 1-3

  • Audit data infrastructure
  • Identify high-impact use cases
  • Select AI tools
  • Train team on AI fundamentals

Phase 2: Pilot

Months 4-6

  • Deploy AI in limited campaigns
  • Measure performance vs. traditional approaches
  • Gather team feedback
  • Document best practices

Phase 3: Scale

Months 7-12

  • Expand AI usage across campaigns
  • Integrate into standard workflows
  • Develop AI-first planning processes
  • Build internal expertise

Phase 4: Innovation

Ongoing

  • Explore emerging AI capabilities
  • Develop proprietary AI applications
  • Share learnings across organization
  • Stay ahead of industry curve
