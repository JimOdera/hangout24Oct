Overcoming Common Challenges

Addressing misconceptions about AI integration in media planning

Challenge

"AI will replace my job"

VS

Reality

AI amplifies strategic roles by eliminating tedious tasks. Demand for skilled strategists is increasing.

Challenge

"Our data isn't good enough"

VS

Reality

Start with the data you have. AI can help identify and fill gaps over time. Perfect data isn't required.

Challenge

"AI tools are too expensive"

VS

Reality

Many powerful AI tools have accessible entry points. The cost of NOT using AI is higher - lost efficiency.

Challenge

"AI lacks creativity and strategy"

VS

Reality

AI doesn't replace human creativity; it enhances it by handling analysis so humans can focus on strategic thinking.
